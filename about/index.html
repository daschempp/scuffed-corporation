02 — the nameyes, on purpose

About the name

“Scuffed” is internet shorthand for rough around the edges — functional, but visibly handmade. It is not a word companies usually reach for, which is roughly why we did.

Version one of everything is scuffed. Most companies handle this by naming themselves something aspirational and hoping nobody looks too closely. We went the other way: put the flaw in the name, then make sure it only ever applies to the surface.

The name is the disclaimer. The engineering is the rebuttal.

“Doesn’t naming yourself after a flaw hurt business?” Almost certainly. We’ll reassess the moment any business turns up to complain.
